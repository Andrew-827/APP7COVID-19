Do you have COVID-19?

The COVID-19 epidemic has shaken the entire world with impacts on health, safety, and the economy. It is important to maintain social distancing as instructed by government officials. However, for essential workers and even for the general public it is important to properly diagnose COVID-19. Are you feeling like you might have symptoms of COVID-19? Answer a few short questions to find out if you should seek treatment or an official test.!
